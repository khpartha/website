Kangshubi, a cherished Manipuri delicacy, blends the earthy richness of black sesame seeds with the natural sweetness of molasses. These tiny seeds are bursting with nutty flavor, healthy fats, protein, and antioxidants. Molasses, or 'Kangshubi' in Manipuri, lends its sweetness and sticky texture to perfectly bind the sesame seeds into a delightful and nutritious treat.
